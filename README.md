## SMB Solutions: An all-in-one app replacing waiters, accountants, and managers.
Fully automated solution for restaurants designed to optimize and streamline daily routine operations. The application handles order management, tracks cooking processes, monitors inventory, generates analytics, and facilitates staff communication. Highly customizable to fit any business needs.

## Architecture

The system consists of three interconnected components:

**Waiter**: A tool for managers and waiters. Replaces notebooks, paper scraps, Excel spreadsheets, and paper shredders.

**Kitchen & Packstation**: Interfaces designed to be displayed on monitors at the chef's and packer's workstations.
